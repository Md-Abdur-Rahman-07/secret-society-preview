Locations & Headquarters

Explore the rumored or known headquarters of famous secret societies around the world. Click on a location to learn more.

Secret societies have always chosen their headquarters carefully, often selecting locations that are steeped in mystery, history, and secrecy. These locations are usually hidden in plain sight, with their true significance known only to those within the organization. Here are a few examples of such secretive places:

The Green Dragon Tavern (Boston) - Known as the "Headquarters of the Revolution," this tavern played a pivotal role in American history. It was a meeting spot for the Sons of Liberty and was involved in planning the Boston Tea Party.

The Old South Meeting House (Boston) - This historic church also served as a meeting space for the Freemasons and played a key role during the American Revolution, notably in planning the Boston Tea Party.

The Boston Athenaeum - One of the oldest independent libraries in the U.S., this location was a gathering place for intellectual societies and housed some of Boston's most influential figures.

The Omni Parker House (Boston) - A historic hotel, it hosted secret meetings and gatherings, including those of the Saturday Club, a group of writers and intellectuals.

Skull and Bones (Yale University) - The infamous society at Yale meets in a windowless brick building known as "The Tomb," located on the university's campus. This society has attracted numerous political elites over the years.

These locations are just a few examples of how secret societies maintain their allure and mystery, often blending into the urban landscape, making it difficult to discern their true purpose.

Freemasons' Hall, London

The Freemasons' Hall in London is a historic building serving as the headquarters of the United Grand Lodge of England. Known for its stunning architecture, it is considered a key landmark for Freemasonry, symbolizing centuries of secrecy and tradition.

Skull and Bones, Yale University

Located on Yale University's campus, the Skull and Bones society is one of America's most mysterious secret groups. Known for its elite membership, it has housed numerous influential figures in politics and business over the decades.

Bilderberg Group, Netherlands

The Bilderberg Group often gathers at exclusive venues like the Bilderberg Hotel in the Netherlands. This organization, shrouded in secrecy, is famous for hosting annual meetings attended by powerful world leaders and business elites.

Bohemian Grove, California

Bohemian Grove, located in the redwood forests of California, is a retreat for the exclusive Bohemian Club. Known for its secretive gatherings, it has hosted some of the world's most influential political and business leaders.
